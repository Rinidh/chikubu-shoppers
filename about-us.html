About Us

Start Up

Our journey began in 2001 with a simple mission: to provide our community with high-quality products at affordable prices. What started as a small family-run store, "Chikubu groceries" along the Chikubu lane. But today, it has grown into a trusted destination for all your shopping needs. Founded by Rinidh Dhokia, our humble beginnings were rooted in the belief that everyone deserves access to essential goods close to home. With hard work, dedication, and the support of our loyal customers, we expanded from a single aisle shop to the vibrant supermarket we are today. Over the years, we have remained committed to our core values of quality, affordability, and excellent customer service.

About Us

Start Up

Our journey began in 2001 with a simple mission: to provide our community with high-quality products at affordable prices. What started as a small family-run store, "Chikubu groceries" along the Chikubu lane. But today, it has grown into a trusted destination for all your shopping needs. Founded by Rinidh Dhokia, our humble beginnings were rooted in the belief that everyone deserves access to essential goods close to home. With hard work, dedication, and the support of our loyal customers, we expanded from a single aisle shop to the vibrant supermarket we are today. Over the years, we have remained committed to our core values of quality, affordability, and excellent customer service. Our story is not just about growth; it’s about staying true to our roots while embracing innovation to better serve you. Thank you for being part of our journey. Together, we look forward to many more years of serving our community!

Our Specialty

Over the years, we’ve built strong partnerships and entered numerous mutual agreements & contracts with trusted suppliers and distributors such as Nestle & Afica Queen and local producers like VALA (U) LTD, Nile Breweries, Lato Dairy producers etc. These relationships allow us to source products directly (without middlemen) and at contractual advantages.
Supporting local farmers and businesses is part of our mission. Not only does this strengthen the community, but it also means fresher products at lower costs.

Our Vision

To be the leading sudivermarket that transforms lives by providing high-quality, affordable products while empowering local communities and fostering sustainable growth.
